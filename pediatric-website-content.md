## 8. Breastfeeding — "Why is Breastfeeding Best?" (स्तनपान ही सर्वोत्तम क्यों है?)

### Important advice for mothers (माताओं के लिए महत्वपूर्ण सलाह)

- Breast milk is rich in nutrients (vitamins, minerals, antibodies) needed for growth after birth.
- It protects against diseases; easily digestible; lowers allergy risk.
- For the first month give only breast milk — no water or other drinks.

### Characteristics of mother's milk (माँ के दूध की विशेषताएं)

1. **Colostrum:** First milk after delivery is yellow and thick; appears in the first week. Richer in protein and infection-fighting factors than mature milk; important for early protection; high in Vitamin A. Give colostrum to the baby — it is very nutritious.
2. **Digestion & immunity:** Easily digestible; gives immunity from birth; more antibodies than other milk; helps protect from diarrhea, pneumonia, ear infections.
3. **Availability:** Always ready and clean; no preparation or heating needed.

### Benefits of breastfeeding (स्तनपान के फायदे)

1. Best nutrition for growth in the first months.
2. Strengthens immunity and protects against infections.
3. Strengthens mother–child bond.
4. Benefits for mother (e.g. postpartum recovery, some long-term health benefits).

### Key message

**"याद रखें माँ का दूध आपके शिशु के लिए सर्वोत्तम व सर्वाधिक किफायती आहार है!"**  
Remember: Mother's milk is the best and most affordable food for your baby.

## 11. Vaccination — Important Notes (Patient Education)

1. **BCG:** A small blister and then a mark are normal after BCG; no special reason needed.
2. **Common side effects:** Mild fever and swelling at injection site can occur.
3. **Efficacy:** No vaccine gives 100% protection; disease can still occur after vaccination.
4. **Reactions:** Sometimes significant reactions need medical attention.

## 13. When to See the Doctor Immediately

**शिशु में इनमें से कोई भी लक्षण दिखने पर तुरन्त डॉक्टर को दिखायें....**  
If any of these symptoms appear in the child, see a doctor immediately:

- तेज-तेज सांस लेना — Rapid breathing
- बच्चे का सुस्त रहना एवं दुध ना पीना — Lethargy or not drinking milk
- समय पर वजन ना बड़ना — Not gaining weight on time
- झटके आना — Convulsions
- पतली पतली दस्त करना — Frequent loose motions
- समय पर चलना वा बोलना ना सीखना — Not walking or speaking on time
- शरीर का नीला पड़ना — Bluish discoloration of the body

## 14. Introduction to Baby Food (शिशु आहार की शुरुआत)

### Core message

- Good nutrition is very important for healthy growth.
- Breast milk meets all calories and nutrients up to 6 months.
- After 6 months, start complementary foods for extra nutrition.

### When to start (कब शुरू करें)

Start at **6 months** when:
- Weight has doubled from birth weight.
- Baby holds head steady and sits with a little support.

### First foods (प्रथम आहार)

- Start with simple cereals: rice (चावल) or ragi (रागी).
- **Allergy:** Introduce one new cereal at a time; wait 3–7 days before adding another. If rash, red streaks, diarrhea, itching, discomfort or vomiting appear, consult the doctor.
- **Preparation:** Mix cereal with milk or water as per pack; start with 1 teaspoon cereal + 4–5 teaspoons milk/water.
- **Do not add:** Sugar (चीनी), honey (शहद), syrup (सिरप), or other sweeteners.

### After 6 months — increase amount

- **Vegetables & fruits:** After cereals, add pureed/strained vegetable soup (सब्जी का सूप) or fruit juice (फलों का जूस). Start with 1–2 large spoons, increase to about 5 large spoons per meal, twice a day.

### Protein sources (प्रोटिन के स्त्रोत)

After cereals, vegetables and fruits are accepted: strained pureed meat, chicken soup, cooked mashed/dried beans, yogurt (दही), fish, boiled egg white.

### Feeding suggestions (खिलाने संबंधी सुझाव)

- Offer breast milk or formula 1–2 hours before solids so baby gets enough milk.
- Early introduction of complementary food helps build healthy eating habits.
- Feed frequently; infants have small stomachs and high energy needs.
